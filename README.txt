# Lochtris
A Modern Tetris Trainer
*just a little side-project, don't mind me, definitely still a WIP*


Practice PCO with shadow guides when you get stuck!

--------------------------------------------------------

## How to Play:

### On the Web:
	https://s66lem.github.io/lochtris/

### By Downloading the Files:
　1. Go To https://github.com/s66lem/ttpc
　2. Click "Clone or Download"
　3. Download the files as a ZIP
　4. Unzip and open index.html in your browser (Firefox, Chrome, IE, etc.) 
　5. Enjoy!

--------------------------------------------------------

## How To Delete:
　Just delete the folder!
